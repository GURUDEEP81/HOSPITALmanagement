Cosmetic Gynaecology


Overview

Infertility treatment focuses on helping couples conceive by addressing reproductive challenges in both men and women. Gynecological laparoscopy is a minimally invasive surgical technique used to diagnose and treat infertility-related conditions. Together, they provide solutions for fertility issues.

Common Conditions
  • Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS): Hormonal imbalance causing irregular ovulation.
  • Endometriosis: Uterine lining tissue growing outside the uterus, affecting fertility.
  • Blocked Fallopian Tubes: Preventing the egg from reaching the uterus
  • Fibroids: Non-cancerous growths in the uterus interfering with conception.
  • Unexplained Infertility: No identifiable cause but difficulties in conceiving.
    • Causes

    Hormonal imbalances, infections, or age-related factors. Chronic health conditions, obesity, or stress. Structural abnormalities of reproductive organs.

    Treatments

    Medications: Ovulation-inducing drugs like Clomiphene or hormonal therapy. Assisted Reproductive Techniques: IVF, IUI, or egg freezing. Laparoscopic Procedures: Removal of fibroids, treating endometriosis, or clearing blocked tubes. Lifestyle Modifications: Weight management and stress reductio

    Precautions

    Maintain a healthy lifestyle and balanced diet. Regular gynecological check-ups to detect issues early. Avoid smoking, excessive alcohol, and environmental toxins.
